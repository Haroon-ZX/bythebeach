About IJsvogel – Flexible Venue at Zilvermeer

Located at the Zilvermeer recreational domain, IJsvogel is a multifunctional venue designed to adapt to different user needs and seasons.

In summer, the space connects directly with the beach atmosphere — offering outdoor activities, social gatherings, and vibrant events.

In winter, IJsvogel transforms into a comfortable indoor environment for meetings, workshops, and cozy social experiences, maintaining activity throughout the year.

Key Features

  • Beach & nature views
  • Flexible layouts (meeting, workshop, event)
  • Easy access & nearby parking
  • Catering & bar services available

Package Capacities

  • Corporate Package: 100 – 300 people
  • Sports Group: 50 – 200 people
  • Event / Social: 250 – 500 people
